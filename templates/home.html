About Us

A Chama is an informal cooperative society that is normally used to pool and invest savings by most people in East Africa and particularly in Kenya. Chama is a Kiswahili word for ‘’group’’ and the Chama phenomenon arose out of the idea of Harambe, which means “all together” in the late 1980s and 1990s. Originally, chamas tended to be exclusively women’s groups, but as chamas started to grow, men started participating as well.
